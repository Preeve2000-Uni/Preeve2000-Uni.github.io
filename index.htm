The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy is a guide to the galaxy for use by hitchhikers. The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy is also a lengthy franchise that has been adapted in endless formats; including multiple hours worth of audio, multiple hours worth of low-budget video, multiple hours of high-budget video featuring many well-known actors, multiple hours of frustration on a text-only computer game, and a trilogy in 5 books consisting of even more text and multiple hours of reading, as well as a cashed-in appendix written by a different person doing a poor attempt at emulating the style of the original author, many years after the original author keeled over.

There has been a recently discovered additional html web format version created by a University student with little coding knowledge, little time, little patience, little imagination to come up with an original story, little ability to emulate the style of the original author, and seemingly, little shame considering how much text lifted wholesale from the books it consists of.

This is the story of that additional html web format version.
